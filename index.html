06 — FAQ

Got
Questions?
Here.

What happens if something breaks? +
All products include 30 days of free support after delivery. After that, annual plans start at R5,000/year. Most issues resolved in 24 hours via remote session. Critical failures get same-day response.
How long does a project take? +
Standard website packages: 5–21 days. Custom builds: 2–6 weeks. We give fixed timelines upfront — no surprises. If we say 5 days, we deliver in 5 days.
Do I need to train my staff? +
No. The whole point is software that works without consultants or training. We deliver with a simple walkthrough. If documentation is needed, we write it. Your team uses it from day one.
Can you work with our existing systems? +
Yes. Most engineering firms use AutoCAD, Civil 3D, Trello, WhatsApp, and municipal portals. We build bridges to these systems. If your data lives somewhere, we can connect to it.
How is pricing structured? +
Fixed price, agreed upfront before work begins. No hourly billing, no scope creep, no surprises. You know exactly what you pay before we write a single line of code.
